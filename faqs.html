Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I request a document?

A: At the home page, click the “Request Document” button or the “Request Document” at the top of the page (navigation bar). Select which document you want to request. Fill the form with an appropriate answer and hit next until you reach step 3. Hit submit. - Note down the transaction id

Q: How do I request an appointment?

A: At the home page, click the “Schedule Appointment” at the top of the page. Fill up the form with appropriate answers and hit submit. - Note down the appointment id

Q: What should I answer in the purpose bar at the appointment schedule?

A: At the purpose bar indicate what you’re going to do, the official you want to meet, etc. Here are few examples: > Meeting, Barangay captain. > document signing, barangay captain

Q: How can I see what is happening with my document (track request)?

A: At the home page, click track request at the top of the page (navigation bar). Click document request button. Fill the search bar with the transaction ID that was sent to you when you submitted your request. Download the document in the download bar.

Q: How can I see the status of my appointment?

A: Click track request at the top of the page (navigation bar). Click appointment schedule button. Fill the search bar with the appointment ID that was sent to you when you submitted your request. Download the document in the download bar.

Q: Why I do not see my document at the download bar (applies to document request and schedule appointment)?

A: If there’s no document present in the download bar, the document is most likely not available yet or your request is rejected.

Q: It hass been three (working) days or more, where is my document?

A: Your document is already rejected. Feel free to request another one or ask at the barangay

Q: After downloading my document, is there anything I still need to do?

A: Yes. your document needs an official dry seal or a sign from a barangay official. You can only get these from the barangay office. Feel free to use our schedule appointment feature to have your document signed at the best time and day.

Q: After submitting my document request or appointment request, what next?

A: You have to wait for a text message or a notification from the website, make sure to not lose your transaction ID that was sent to you.
